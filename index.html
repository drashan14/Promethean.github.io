About The Event

The Promethean Club

The club is an initiative to help the students with the flair of writing and speaking. Language empowers an individual in sharing information, improving interpersonal skills and sensitizing people all over the world. The students are exposed to creative writing and speaking. They are encouraged to contribute their writings to the college Magazines and for social causes. It also helps the students to interact through competitions and gain confidence.

Objectives of the Club

1. To use the productive and receptive skills in a language creatively using one's own perspective

2.To be able to relate to the culture and society.

3.To be able to articulate and express ideas effectively.

4.Develop effective writing skills with an ability to influence the world through artistic values.
